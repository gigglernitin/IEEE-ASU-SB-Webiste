    • What is IEEE Career "IELTS Orientation session"?!

      IEEE is opening the opportunity to enhance and strengthen your technical skills through affording variety of technical packages and orientation sessions concerning the real world jobs with zero fees…

      -IELTS Orientation session:

      -The British Council is willing to offer free IELTS Information session to University students to raise awareness of IELTS .This free 90-minute session will help students understand the IELTS Writing, IELTS reading, IELTS Speaking and IELTS Listening modules format ,how it is scored , and how to prepare effectively. The session will be delivered by an experienced British Council English Language instructor with expertise on IELTS test format and preparation. Students will have the chance to ask questions about the IELTS exam and discuss their concerns..
